It all started Christmas Morning 1989 me and my little brother would wake up to a life changing gift even though we wouldn't know it at the time. Santa Clause had given us Prodigy it was a disk that would allow us to connect to people all over the world. At the time I had no clue just what the web would bring and the amount of time I would spend on "bulletin boards". Fast Forward a few years to senior year of highschool (1998) I learned HTML and build my 1st website.

After returning from a study abroad program in Italy (art history) I had the urge to start building again, so I decided to sign up for a web design class at the local JC. From there I would build a few more sites for friends and then move into a new job of helping create an online reference database for a sound distributor. After that project ended in the early 2000's I would move on to the telecom industry.

Shortly after starting my career in the telecom industry as a phone rep, I was hired to work within the finance department on a project for automating internal processes and document retention. From there I would assist creating a database that would allow for offline reps to track work then integrate it into the company wide billing systems.

When those projects ended I moved on to work on a reference content project that would take all the company policies and procedures through a complete overhaul and began creatingsmart tools that would integrate company polices and procedure within company specific systems used by reps throughout the company. In this project I serve as one of the main project managers for all rewrites of policies and procedures working with various teams throughout the company.

This project lead to the creation of smart guides that utilize iLog (business rule engine) and allowed for process to become automated for customers. The project resulted in reducing company overhead by $25 million a year with in customer contact centers.
